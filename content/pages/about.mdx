Tsang Ka-Lung(in Cantonese, Zeng Jia-long in Mandarin) is a software engineer at ByteDance, specializing in Payment Marketing for [Douyin Pay](https://www.douyinpay.com/).

From 2023 to 2024, he contributed to the mobile pages of [Tiktok Shop](https://shop.tiktok.com/business/en).

Before that, he was instrumental in developing ByteDance's hybrid framework for mobile platforms. Earlier in his career, he focused on the runtime and build systems for mini programs at Ant Group.

He obtained a bachelor's degree in engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications.
