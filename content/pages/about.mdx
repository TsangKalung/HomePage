Tsang Ka-Lung(in Cantonese, Zeng Jia-long in Mandarin) is a software engineer at Zoom who works on [Zoom Docs](https://www.zoom.com/en/products/collaborative-docs-new/).

From 2022 to August 2025, he spent three years at ByteDance:

- Contributed to the mobile pages of [Tiktok Shop](https://shop.tiktok.com/business/en) and [Douyin Pay](https://www.douyinpay.com/).
- Developed ByteDance's hybrid framework for mobile platforms.

Prior to that, from 2021 to 2022, he focused on runtime and build systems for mini programs at [Ant Group](https://www.antgroup.com/en).

He obtained a bachelor's degree in engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (2017 - 2021). His time at university was a special chapter in his life, marked by both ambition and a touch of melancholy.

---

He is passionate about computer science, languages, investing, and crypto. In his spare time, he enjoys reading, photography, and traveling the world.
